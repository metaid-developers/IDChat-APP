IDChat Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-15
Important Notice:

Please read this IDChat Privacy Policy carefully to understand how we collect, use, store, and share your information. By using IDChat or continuing to use it after updates, you agree to this policy (including updates). If you disagree, please stop using IDChat. For questions, contact us at devs@idchat.io.

Introduction

IDChat prioritizes your privacy and security. Built on Bitcoin and powered by MetaID, we use robust security measures to prevent data leaks, offering decentralized control. We collect only necessary data, ensuring transparency and compliance with laws.

1. Information We Collect

  1. Usage Data:

    When you use IDChat, we automatically record device type, IP address, and app usage logs to ensure functionality.

  2. Wallet Data:

    We store wallet addresses and on-chain chat data you create or import, secured by Bitcoin-level encryption.

  3. Consent:

    We will notify you and seek consent for any additional data collection.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and improve IDChat services.
  • Ensure security (e.g., fraud detection, backups).
  • Optimize performance based on usage patterns.

Data is not linked to your identity and is used only for the above purposes unless you consent otherwise.

3. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your personal information except:

  • To deliver IDChat services (e.g., node operations).
  • With your consent or as required by law (e.g., for public safety or legal investigations).
  • In case of mergers, the new entity will honor this policy.

We use strict confidentiality agreements with any third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored on the Bitcoin blockchain, encrypted with end-to-end methods.
  • We use encryption and access controls to protect your information.
  • In case of a security breach, we will notify you via app announcements.
  • Data is retained only as long as needed for service purposes, per legal requirements.

5. Your Data Management

  • Wallet Creation: Users must be 18+ or have legal capacity to create a wallet.
  • Control: You manage your wallet password, private key, or mnemonic. IDChat cannot recover lost credentials due to its decentralized nature.
  • Access: You can request to view or correct your data by contacting us.
  • Rights: You may object to automated decisions affecting your rights; we’ll provide explanations.

6. Children’s Privacy

IDChat is not for users under 18. If a minor uses it without parental consent, we will remove their data after consulting guardians.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to improve services, notifying you via https://idchat.io or in-app. Major changes (e.g., data use purpose) will be highlighted. Old versions are archived for review.

8. Contact Us

For concerns or inquiries, email devs@idchat.io. We aim to respond within 15 days.
